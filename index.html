About HK-FUSION

HK-FUSION is an advanced energy research corporation focused on developing Artificial Sun technology through controlled nuclear fusion. Our mission is to provide unlimited, clean, and carbon-free energy for the next century of humanity.

Core Fusion Technologies

Tokamak Fusion

Magnetic confinement plasma systems for sustained fusion reactions.

Laser Inertial Fusion

High-energy laser compression systems for ignition experiments.

Plasma Engineering

Advanced plasma stability control and magnetic field optimization.

Superconducting Magnets

Next-generation high-temperature superconducting magnet systems.

Fusion Materials Science

Radiation-resistant materials for reactor longevity and safety.

Grid Integration Systems

Commercial fusion power integration into global energy infrastructure.

Commercial Roadmap

  • Phase 1 – Plasma Ignition Validation
  • Phase 2 – Net Energy Gain Achievement
  • Phase 3 – Prototype Reactor Deployment
  • Phase 4 – Commercial Fusion Power Plants

Global Impact

Fusion energy eliminates carbon emissions, reduces geopolitical energy conflicts, and provides sustainable power for AI, robotics, and space industries.
